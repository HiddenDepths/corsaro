ABOUT US

We are a traditional Sardinian and Sicilian cucina, nestled on Gosforth High Street. With a charming mediterranean ambience our cosy restaurant transports you to the heart of Italy. Using only locally sourced produce, our seafood is all fresh, our varied menu contains vegetarian and gluten free options.
Our wines are from our homeland, our pistachio crema liquor is a little taste of heaven, whilst Corsaro's home-made classic or pistachio tiramisu is belissimo.
Claudio and Chef Alberto look forward to welcoming you and your friends soon.
